Hypothesis Testing

The hypothesis test calculator helps you perform various statistical hypotheses, which are mentioned below.

Conformity tests of a parameter

Perform one-sample test (Z test, T test and Chi-squared test) on the population's mean, variance or proportion.

Comparison tests

Perform two-sample comparison test (Z test, T test and Fisher test) on the population's mean, variance or proportion.

Independance tests

Perform parametric and non parametric independance tests on two random variables

Equality of probability distributions test

Test the equality of some theoritical distribution with a sample's distribution

ANOVA test

Perform Bartlett test and then the ANOVA on your samples
